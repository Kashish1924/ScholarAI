About ScholarAI

A scholarship discovery platform built to reduce search chaos.

ScholarAI is designed for Indian B.Tech students who need a cleaner, faster way to discover scholarships without jumping across multiple websites. The platform uses structured backend rules for discovery, scoring, filtering, and deadline awareness.

Product Principles

  • Backend logic stays the source of truth
  • AI remains optional and explainable
  • Students browse without mandatory login
  • Admins manage clean, validated scholarship data

Discovery

Find relevant scholarships faster

Students can search by keyword, income, state, category, branch, deadline, and scholarship type from one unified interface.

Eligibility

Deterministic scoring over guesses

Recommendation scores come from Python business rules, not AI assumptions, so the platform stays transparent and testable.

Operations

Admin workflows that scale

The admin side supports scholarship CRUD, content publishing, CSV data operations, analytics, and now contact-message review.
