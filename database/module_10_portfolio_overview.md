The last mile of a strong project is communication. Good engineering work becomes much more valuable when it is easy to explain, demo, publish, and defend in interviews.

## Interview Mindset

- Explain why the architecture is modular
- Emphasize deterministic backend logic for eligibility
- Show that AI is optional and intentionally separated
- Mention tests and deployment readiness to demonstrate project maturity
